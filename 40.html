Product Launches
More NFT API Functionality
We’ve launched a few exciting updates to our NFT API:

getNFTsForCollection is live:
This endpoint will allow devs to get all tokens for a collection / contract. Check out the docs here!

Use ENS names in owner field for getNFTs:
We also have seen that users are eager to utilize their ens name in the owner parameter. So we built this functionality!

Handling any token ID format:
Based on user demand, we now accept token IDs to the getNFTMetadata api in the form of an integer, a hex string or an extended hex string, and regardless of the format you send us, you will always access the same metadata.

Flow Contract Filters on getNFTs API:
The Flow getNFTs API now supports parameters (contractAddresses[] and contractNames[] to filter NFTs by contract for an owner in the getNFTs endpoint. Check out the docs!

Flow New Contract Support
We are constantly working on increasing contract coverage support on our Flow NFT API! Last week we added support for 10 new contracts (including Rarible) on Flow Mainnet and 3 new contracts on Flow Testnet. Check out the full list!

Meet the team
Hi everyone! My name is Gus and I’m just finishing up my first month working on the Recruiting team at Alchemy. My job is pretty self-explanatory: I’m going to be helping Alchemy continue to build a world-class team so we deliver an amazing product to our customers. The day-to-day of it all is a little less self-explanatory, but that’s what makes it fun 🙂

What was your aha moment for Web3?
I’ve had a few of them over the last couple of years (and I feel like I have a few of them everyday here at Alchemy!). The biggest one for me was realizing the potential applications of smart contracts across so many different industries. It feels like there are endless practical solutions for that type of product. More recently, I’ve been enjoying learning about DAOs and thinking about different ways that they can be used.

What were you up to prior to joining Alchemy?
I most recently spent the last 6.5 years at HubSpot where I helped build, and was eventually lucky enough to lead, the Tech Recruiting organization. I knew that I wanted to join an amazing start-up when I decided to leave HubSpot and Alchemy checked all the boxes for me. I learned a ton during my time there and I’m super excited to bring the best of that to Alchemy.

What are you most excited about?
It’s cliche, but I’m a people person. So it has to be the people. The awesome thing though is that this extends out beyond the people inside Alchemy (who are awesome, don’t get me wrong). But the whole community surrounding Web3 is so supportive and enthusiastic that I’m just super excited to see what everyone builds. I’m feeling really lucky to play my small role in pushing us all forward.
