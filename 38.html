Meet the Team
 
P. S. We’re hiring like crazy!
 
 
Suzanne Beinfeld
 
Product Marketer at Alchemy 👋
 
Hi everyone! I’m Suzanne and this is my first week as a Product Marketer at Alchemy. My job will be to help drive go-to-market for our new product launches, and to ensure that that we’re building and positioning our products in ways that resonate with our partners. I’m thrilled to be joining the Alchemy team and the Web3 movement!
 
What was your aha moment for Web3?
 
I feel like I’m having an aha moment every day! A specific one that comes to mind is when I was interviewing for this role, I was asked to do a case study on a (at the time) hypothetical launch of an NFT API. For the first time in years, I found myself in "deep work" — at my computer past midnight, with no sense of how much time had passed, going down the rabbit hole of learning about this ecosystem. I knew that if this work makes me this engaged, and there’s a chance I could make it my full time gig, I needed to make it happen.
 
What were you up to prior to joining Alchemy?
 
Most recently, I worked as a Product Marketer on Meta’s Developer Platform. Meta’s Developer Platform is the central team supporting over 500 product teams’ APIs and Platform Products. In my time on the team, I focused on two primary initiatives: first, the Platform’s integrity efforts and ensuring we were building APIs in safe, sustainable ways. And second, building out the set of products to enable sharing content from third-party apps and websites to Instagram and Facebook.
 
What are you most excited about working on?
 
I can’t choose just one thing so I will chose my top two. First, the team! I was blown away by the people I met at Alchemy during the interview process. Each person on the team is a uniquely remarkable combination of intelligent, humble, curious and kind. I’m so excited to work with them. Second, the opportunity to be a Product Marketer in the Web3 space feels particularly enticing. Web3 is on the precipice of moving from contrarian to conventional and as a Product Marketer, I love the idea of being a translator in the space, helping to make that transition happen.
