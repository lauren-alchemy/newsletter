Meet the Team
 
P. S. We’re hiring like crazy!
 
 
Hi everyone! I’m Aniket and I’ve been an entrepreneur now for ~2 years, running my own blockchain company. I code, I sell, I do motivational speeches and by now I can also probably be a good host for your wedding! I love losing myself in impactful work, evidenced by writing this at 2 am India time. IIT → Goldman Sachs → Founder → DevRel at Alchemy. Hoping to make the most of my time at this rocketship!
 
What was your aha moment for Web3?
 
It was 4 am in the morning and I learned that Beeple sold his NFT for $69 million. Spent the next 6 hours and the next several months, reading and coding. In that time, I fell in love with the fact that Web3 is wildly ambitious, mind-bending, and deeply algorithmic. I haven’t looked back since and plan to spend the next decade pioneering the space.

 
What led you to Alchemy?
 
Nikil’s physique flex in NYTimes. JK. I knew Alchemy would be a foundational company from the first time I heard about it. Alchemy wasn’t hiring in India so I reached out to Nikil on Twitter. Got a response, went through the hiring process, and that’s how Alchemy got one of its first international hires!
 
What are you most excited about working on?
 
To work with Alchemy’s exceptionally talented team and help Alchemy grow 10x in Asia.
