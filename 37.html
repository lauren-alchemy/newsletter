Meet the Team
 
 
Vitto Rivabella
Newest Developer Advocate at Alchemy 👋
 
Heyo! Vitto here, from Italy! The latest addition to the Alchemy’s team!
My job? Onboard developers in the Web3 space by creating stunning resources and content.
 
What was your aha moment for Web3?
 
When I met a group of women-only Afghan researchers that had been able to escape the war thanks to bitcoin, the coin they were paid with for their job. The Afghan currency dropped to below 0.0095 USD in just a few months, halving its value because of war. It was hard for the country’s citizens to do any business with foreign states, and impossible to leave the country with all your life savings now worth a handful of USD. Bitcoin of course didn’t suffer any effect of the Afghan war, maintaining its value and allowing them to leave the country.
 
Why did you join Alchemy?
 
To make an impact, to educate people, to join the top minds in the space, to learn and help. That’s what Alchemy is all about, amazing tech created by stunning people, I couldn’t not join.
 
What are you most excited about working on?
 
Web3.University! Free web3 education from top minds in the Blockchain development space. Isn’t it so cool?
